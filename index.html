Kush

I am a PhD researcher in Computer Science, working at UGent-IDLab in the solid project at the KNOWS team. I focus upon the scalability of the solid project by working towards aggregation of the data stored in solid pods. My research is focused around stream processing, aggregation and decentralization. I hold a master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Lyon.
